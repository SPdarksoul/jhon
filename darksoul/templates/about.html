About Us

During the lockdown, I developed a keen interest in photography. I would capture images of the sky, birds, and trees from my rooftop, and then edit them using my phone. After the COVID-19 restrictions eased, I enrolled at Evolution Image Academy to enhance my skills and broaden my knowledge. I delved deeper into various aspects,

including Photoshop editing, the golden rule, picture composition, white balance, studio lighting, and much more. I consider myself fortunate to have taken this path. Subsequently, I started sharing my work on Instagram, where people responded positively, appreciating the beauty of my photography.

My Instagram page, "Tilting the Perspective," aptly reflects my approach. I aim to capture subjects from unique angles and perspectives, adding a touch of artistic beauty to each image. Photography enables me to convey my vision through the captivating faces and streets I capture in the moment. Over time, I have collaborated with esteemed brands such as L'Oreal, LA Weddings, Lakme Academy, Narang Ethnics, Strictly Organics, and many others.

My expertise spans various photography genres, including portrait, fashion, maternity, product, and more. To refine my craft, I utilize software such as Photoshop and VN applications for editing, ensuring every output I provide satisfies my clients' expectations. Drawing from my experience as a lead actress in two Punjabi songs and one dubbed old Hindi song, I understand the emotions and needs of the person in front of the camera. This understanding allows me to create a comfortable and conducive environment during the photo shoots.
